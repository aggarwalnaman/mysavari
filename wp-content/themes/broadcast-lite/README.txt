== Description ==

Broadcast Lite was built specifically for flawless integration with twitch.tv, straight out the box. Let Broadcast be the home for all your twitch stream news and information. Use it to blog, make announcements, let users keep track of your schedule and see a live countdown to your next stream and more!

Broadcast Lite is distributed under the terms of the GNU GPL

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Themes and click the 'Add New' button.
2. Type in Broadcast Lite in the search form and press the 'Enter' key on your keyboard.
3. Click on the 'Activate' button to use your new theme right away.
5. Navigate to Appearance > Customize in your admin panel and customize to taste.
